https://api.apollo.io/api/v1/contact_stages

Use the List Contact Stages endpoint to retrieve the IDs for the available contact stages in your team's Apollo account.

Contact stage IDs can be used to update invidual contacts and update the contact stages for multiple contacts via the Apollo API.
